The rules are simple!

You have 18 tiles in total.

First select a difficulty level then press the RESTART button.

Now select a tile and memorize it.

Select another tile to try and find it's pair.

Once you have selected a pair successfully, the cards will flip over to the green tile.

If you get it wrong they will flip back to the reverse tile image so you can try again.

When all the tiles have been paired within the required amount of turns, you will be set free!!!

To try again, select a difficulty then hit the RESTART button.

Test your skills on the Ultra difficulty!
